How to Use EA SafariRoutes

Welcome to the future of East African travel. Follow these simple steps to secure your journey.

1. Find Your Route

Browse available SGR and Bus routes across Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, and Rwanda on our home page.

2. Get Your SafariRoutes Code

Click "Get Code" on your chosen trip. Enter your name as it appears on your ID. You will receive a unique tracking code (e.g., SR-2026-K-9A3B7C).

3. Pay via Mobile Money

Present your code at the transport booking office. Pay the official ticket price directly to the operator using M-PESA, Airtel Money, or MTN.

4. On-the-Ground Support

If you face any issues, look for our Regional Representatives at the station. They will ensure your code is accepted instantly.
