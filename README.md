# OSARE - East Africa Transit Super Search

Osare is a unified transit search and booking platform for East Africa that aggregates trains, buses, and informal transport into a single real-time interface.

## The Vision
In the rapidly evolving landscape of East African mobility, information is often fragmented. OSARE bridges this gap by providing a centralized digital gateway for all transport modes—from the Standard Gauge Railway (SGR) to regional bus lines and local Matatu networks.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Unified Aggregation**: Access SGR, long-distance buses, and matatus in one place.
- **Transparency**: A clear 5% service fee on top of official fares—no hidden costs for travelers or operators.
- **Reliability**: The "SafariRoutes Code" system ensures "No Payment, No Code," guaranteeing security for every transaction.
- **Ground Presence**: Human-supported logistics through our regional representatives in Kisumu, Nairobi, Dar es Salaam, and Kampala.

## Business Tiers
1. 🚄 **SafariRoutes**: Professional intercity ticketing for SGR and major bus lines.
2. 🚌 **Jakasipul Hub**: Real-time tracking and information for informal commuter transport.
3. ✈️ **Aviation Hub**: High-end charter services and safari circuit planning.

## Technical Foundation
- **Backend**: FastAPI (Python) optimized for low-latency search.
- **Database**: Neon (Serverless PostgreSQL) for robust, scale-to-zero data management.
- **Security**: Cryptographic hashing for ticket verification and anti-tamper protection.
