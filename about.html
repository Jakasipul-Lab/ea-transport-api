About EA SafariRoutes

EA SafariRoutes was created to simplify how people access travel services across East Africa. Many travelers struggle to find clear and reliable transport options.

The platform brings together train, bus, and private transport routes in one place, making it easy to explore and book travel quickly and efficiently.

EA SafariRoutes also uses data tracking to improve services and ensure better travel decisions. It is designed as a modern digital solution for travel logistics in the region.
