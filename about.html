About EA SafariRoutes

EA SafariRoutes was founded to simplify access to travel and transport services across East Africa. The platform connects travelers with train, bus, and private transport providers through a unified system.

The goal is to remove complexity from travel booking and provide a seamless, reliable, and efficient experience for users. By integrating multiple transport options, EA SafariRoutes becomes a central gateway for mobility.
