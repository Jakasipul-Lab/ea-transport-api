# EA Safariroute: Pitch Deck Outline

## 1. The Problem
- Inter-city travel (SGR, regional buses) in Kenya, Tanzania, and Uganda is hard to book for non-locals and tourists.
- Travelers lack a single, reliable platform to browse all routes and compare prices.
- Many transport companies lose out on potential customers who don't want to deal with complex booking or unofficial "middlemen."

## 2. Our Solution: EA Safariroute
- A unified, "local-first" booking app for SGR and major bus lines across East Africa.
- **The Safariroute Code**: A unique, immutable tracking code for every passenger.
- Seamless lead generation for transport companies.

## 3. Our Unique Niche
- We don't handle the cash. We handle the **leads**.
- High-trust model: The traveler pays the company directly, and the company verifies the sale using our tracking code.

## 4. Business Model
- **Commission**: 5% per ticket sold.
- **Zero Risk**: Transport companies only pay for verified, completed bookings.
- **Scalability**: Starting with high-traffic rail and bus routes, then expanding to specialized safari charters.

## 5. Why Partner with Us?
- Access to a broader digital market (especially tech-savvy locals and international visitors).
- Reliable monthly commission reports for clear auditing.
- No technical integration required on the company's side—just verify our unique code!

## 6. The Roadmap
- **Phase 1**: Populate high-traffic SGR & Bus routes (Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda).
- **Phase 2**: Launch pilot partnerships with 3-5 major bus operators.
- **Phase 3**: Integrate premium "Small Plane" safari charter hiring.
